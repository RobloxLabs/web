Simple Modal

Simple modals are for modals that provide a simple message, prompt, warning, confirmation, etc. The typical format is header, body, image, buttons, and footer.

Adding callbacks on modal close:

You can add callbacks when a modal is closed by chaining the modal "open" call and listening for either a promise resolve or promis reject. The "actionBtn" is tied to a promise "resolve" while the "closeBtn" and "neutralBtn" are tied to promise "reject".

Complex Modal

Complex Modals are any modal that you cannot accomplish by using the simple modal feature. These are modals with other elements than just text and image. You will also be creating your own template and controller to handle logic inside the modal.
